Honey chilly chicken pops #indian_recepies step by step

  1. Marinate the chicken with black paper salt corn flour (add little water too) set aside for 20mints (cut the chicken into small cubes).
  2. Heat oil fry the chicken.
  3. Take a pan add 1 tbs oil add chopped garlic add all the sauce above. Saute for 1 minute add little water then add honey. At the end add chicken and mix well..
  4. Turn off the flame. Garnish the chicken with coriander, spring onions and sesame seeds. serve the chicken hot..
