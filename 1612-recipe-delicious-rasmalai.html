Ingredients of Rasmalai

  1. It's 2 litres of full fat milk.
  2. Prepare 3 cups of sugar.
  3. Prepare 2 of lemon.
  4. It's 8 cups of milk.
  5. You need 4-5 of green cardamom.
  6. You need 1/3 tsp of turmeric powder.
  7. It's of Some dry fruits.
  8. You need 1 of fine cotton cloth.

Rasmalai step by step

  1. First for making rasgulla boil 1 litre milk in a pan. Squeeze lemon juice in a bowl and add some water. Now pour it in the boiling milk. Stir continuously till it starts curdling. When curdled properly and u see greenish whey then switch off the flame and drain and strain the whey using a cotton cloth. Wash the chhena 2 time using water and squeeze it properly. Keep chhena for some time till becomes dry..
  2. Boil 8 cups of water in another pan. Add 2 cups of sugar and Green cardamom..
  3. Now transfer chhena into a plate and knead to make a dough. Make small balls and put it in the boiling water slowly. Cover it for 20-25mins. It will be doubled in size..
  4. Then boil rest of the milk in a pan to make rabdi. Stir continuously. Add 1 cup sugar and 1/3 tsp turmeric powder. When it becomes thick switch off the flame..
  5. Then bring out rasgulla one by one into another bowl. Pour rabdi over it. Garnish with dryfruits. Serve cool..
