Spaghetti Aglio e Olio e Pepperchino instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in the skillet.
  2. Sauté garlic until lightly browned.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Remove pan from heat and add pasta.
  5. Add parmesan, parsley, and lemon juice and mix.
  6. Serve with more parmesan and parsley on top.
  7. Enjoy.
