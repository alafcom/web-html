Ingredients of Creamy fruit custard

  1. It's 1 of lt full fat milk.
  2. Prepare 1 bowl of cold milk.
  3. Prepare 4-5 tsp of custard powder.
  4. Prepare of Sugar as per your taste.
  5. It's of Garnishing.
  6. You need 1/2 of ripe mango chopped.
  7. Prepare 1 bowl of grapes cut into half.
  8. You need 2 of banana cut into thin slices.
  9. It's 1 of /2 cup pomegranate kernels.
  10. It's 2 tsp of chironji seeds.

  3. After 4-5 mins switch off the gas cool it down for 4-5 hours (fridge), after cooling down grind it completely it become creamy..
