Ingredients of Red velvet cookie cake

  1. You need 1/2 cup of refined flour.
  2. It's 3 tbsp of butter.
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp of castor sugar.
  4. It's 1 tsp of red gel colour.

Red velvet cookie cake instructions

  1. Take a bowl add butter, sugar and whisk well.
  2. Add refined flour, colour and whisk till well combined.
  3. Make a dough using spatula.
  4. Refrigerate for half hour.
  5. Make small balls out of it.
  6. Bake at 180 degree celcius for 25 minutes.
  7. Serve.
