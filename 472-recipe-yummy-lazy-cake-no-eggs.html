Ingredients of Lazy cake no eggs

  1. Prepare 400 grams of tea biscuits.
  2. Prepare 14 oz of sweetened condensed milk.
  3. Prepare Cup of marshmallow.
  4. It's Cup of coconut.
  5. You need Cup of pistachio chopped.
  6. You need 4 tablespoon of butter unsalted.
  7. You need 1 envelope of gelatin.
  8. It's 2 tablespoon of Hershey cocoa.

Lazy cake no eggs step by step

  1. Brake the biscuit in big bowl.
  2. On medium heat melt the butter with the cocoa add the marshmallows,coconut and pistachio chopped cook for a minute then add condensed-milk and the envelope gelatin mix well.
  3. Add the mix to the biscuit use loaf dish pressed firmly cooled an hour enjoy.
