Ingredients of Paneer aur Pyaaz ke pakore/ Fritters

  1. Prepare of Onion 2 diced.
  2. You need of Paneer cubes 5-6.
  3. You need of Besan (Gram Flour).
  4. Prepare of Salt as per taste.
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon of Red Chilli powder.
  6. You need of Chaat masala half tbspoon.
  7. You need 1 tablespoon of Crushed Anardana.
  8. Prepare of Turmeric powder half tbspoon.
  9. It's of Coriander powder half tbspoon.
  10. Prepare of Water half cup.
  11. You need 5 cups of Oil.

Paneer aur Pyaaz ke pakore/ Fritters step by step

  1. Take a bowl.....Add Besan and salt, turmeric powder and red chilli and mix it well.
  2. Now add chat masala, Coriander powder and mix it again.
  3. Now add water and make a thick paste and mix all the ingredients well.
  4. Now add onions and paneer cubes and again mix all well and keep it for 1 hour for marination.
  5. Now Fry them in hot oil.
  6. Fry them deep and Serve hot with chutney and tea.
  7. Serve hot w.
