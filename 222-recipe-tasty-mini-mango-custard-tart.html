Recipe: Tasty Mini Mango Custard Tart

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Mini Mango Custard Tart.

You can cook Mini Mango Custard Tart using 7 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Mini Mango Custard Tart

  1. Prepare 1 Packet of Parle-g Biscuit.
  2. It's 3 tbsp of Butter(Melted).
  3. It's 1 of & 1/4 cup Milk.
  4. Prepare 3 tsp of Sugar.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of Custard Powder.
  6. It's 1 of Ripe Mango(Alphanso).
  7. You need 4-5 of saffron Strands.

Mini Mango Custard Tart instructions

  1. In a mixture jar, grind the Parle-G biscuit. Add butter to it and mix it well..
  2. Line your cupcake pan with cupcake liners, add the biscuit mixture in it and flatten it. refrigerate it for 3 to 4 hrs..
  3. Now boil 1 cup of milk. Add Sugar and saffron strands to it. While milk is heating up, take 1/4 cup milk and add 1 tbsp custard powder to it. Make sure there should be no lumps in custard mix..
  4. Add the custard paste to the milk once it boils. Keep stiring it. once milk has turned thicken,let it cool for 5 minutes and put cling wrap on the milk so that there should be no malai in the milk. refrigerate it for 3 to 4 hours..
  5. Now take a mango, cut it in a thin slices and put them in a straight line and make a flower from it..
  6. While serving. demould the biscuit tart from cupcake liners and add custard mixture to it. On the top gently put mango flower..
  7. Your no bake mango tart is ready to serve..
