Ingredients of Tropical Green Smoothie

  1. It's 1/3 cup of coconut milk (more for a thinner smoothie).
  2. Prepare 1 of medium banana.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of chopped pineapple pieces.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of chopped mango pieces.
  5. It's 1 of heaping cup fresh kale (or spinach).
  6. It's 1/2 tsp. of ground flax seed (optional).
  7. You need 1 tsp. of honey (optional).
