Ingredients of Keto Friendly Savory Biscotti

  1. Prepare 2/3 cup of almond flour.
  2. Prepare 2 table spoon of flax seed meal.
  3. You need 2 tbs of grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of coconut flour.
  5. You need 1 tsp of garlic powder.
  6. Prepare 3/4 tsp of baking powder.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  8. It's to taste of Salt and pepper.
  9. It's 2 tbs of olive oil.
  10. It's 2 of eggs.

Keto Friendly Savory Biscotti step by step

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.line a baking sheet with parchment paper..
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together : almond flour, flaxseed meal, Parmesan, coconut flour, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, baking powder, salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk in the oil and in the eggs, let the batter sit 3 mins. To thicken.
  4. Pour the batter out onto prepared tray and spread it out to a rectangle, bake 10 Minutes and remove the loaf from oven, use a sharp knife to slice to loaf on a diagonal into 1/2 inch thick slices, (I had 9 slices) arrange the biscottis on the tray bake until golden, about 25 minutes, (flip them halfway).
  5. Next time I will use olives..
