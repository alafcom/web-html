Ingredients of Aam ka achar

  1. Prepare 500 grams of raw mangoes washed dry and cut into small cubes.
  2. Prepare 5-6 tsp of mustard oil.
  3. It's 2 tsp of yellow mustard.
  4. You need 1 tsp of fenugreek seeds.
  5. It's 1 tsp of fennel seeds.
  6. It's 1 tsp of nigella seeds.
  7. You need 1 tsp of turmeric powder.
  8. Prepare of Salt as per your taste.
  9. It's 2 tsp of red chilli powder.
  10. You need 1 tsp of asafoedita.

Aam ka achar step by step

  1. Heat a tadka pan heat mustard oil switch off the gas and put asafoedita on it..cool it completely..
  2. Grind the yellow mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Take a large bowl put mango cubes add grind powder, fennel seeds, nigella seeds, Turmeric powder, salt, chilli powder and mustard oil..
