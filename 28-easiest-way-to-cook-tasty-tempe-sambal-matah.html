Ingredients of Tempe Sambal Matah

  1. You need of TEMPE:.
  2. It's 1 block of tempe (approx. 200 g).
  3. It's of All-purpose flour.
  4. It's of Rice flour.
  5. You need of Garlic powder.
  6. Prepare of Water.
  7. You need of Salt pepper.
  8. Prepare of Cooking oil.
  9. You need of SAMBAL MATAH:.
  10. You need 5 of bird chilles.
  11. Prepare 5 of red chillies.
  12. You need 5 of shallots.
  13. You need 2 of lemongrass (white part inside).
  14. You need 4 of kaffir lime leaves.
  15. You need 1 of lime (juice).
  16. You need 1-2 tbsp of oil (coconut /vegetable oil).
  17. Prepare of Salt.
  18. It's of Sugar.

Tempe Sambal Matah step by step

  1. Cut tempe into thin slice (around 1 cm thickness).
  2. Ratio of water:all-purpose flour:rice flour is 4:2:1.
  3. Combine flour, garlic powder, salt, pepper and water until batter is form..
  4. Dip tempe into the batter and drop into hot oil until golden brown. Put aside..
  5. Slice thinly all the sambal matah ingredients and press down the chillies mixture with spoon..
  6. Heat the oil in a small frying pan over low heat..
  7. Pour and stir the hot oil into the chillies mixture. It's ready to serve..
