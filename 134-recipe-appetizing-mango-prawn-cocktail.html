Ingredients of Mango prawn cocktail

  1. It's 1 of medium size ripe mango.
  2. It's 1 of small size raw mango.
  3. You need 200 grams of small prawn.
  4. It's 1 cup of plain mayonnaise.
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp of mustard sauce.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of worcestershire sauce.
  7. You need 1 tbsp of capsico sauce.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of tomato sauce.
  9. You need 1 of lemon.
  10. It's 1 tsp of black pepper powder.
  11. Prepare of Salt as per taste.

Mango prawn cocktail step by step

  1. Keep ready all the ingredients..
  2. Deshell & devein the prawns, wash well & boil with some salt & water. Then keep the boiled prawns marinated with lemon juice & ½ tsp black pepper powder..
  3. Cut the ripe mango & raw mango into halves, make pulp with one half & cut the other halves into long thick strips..
  4. In a bowl take the plain mayonnaise, mustard sauce, worcestershire sauce, capsico sauce, tomato sauce, ripe mango pulp, raw mango pulp, the remaining ½ tsp black pepper powder & salt as per taste. Mix it all together very well. Then keep aside 10-12 boiled marinated prawns & put the rest of the prawns in this mix..
  5. Keep the mixture in the refrigerator for a while..
  6. Then pour the cool mixture in serving glasses of your choice & garnish with the long thick strips of ripe mango & raw mango & the marinated prawns that had been kept aside..
  7. Mango Prawn Cocktail is ready to serve..
