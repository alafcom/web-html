Parmesan Apple Pie.

You can cook Parmesan Apple Pie using 12 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Parmesan Apple Pie

  1. It's of APPLE FILLING.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of ground cinnamon.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of brown sugar.
  4. It's 5 small of apple with skin.
  5. Prepare of PARMESAN CRUST.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  7. You need 1 cup of self raising flour.
  8. You need 1 cup of browm sugar.
  9. You need 2 small of eggs.
  10. Prepare 120 grams of butter.
  11. Prepare of ADDITIONAL TOPPING.
  12. It's 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese.

Parmesan Apple Pie step by step

  1. On a baking dish 8/8 put apple with brown sugar and cinnamon powder and mix well.
  2. In a mixing bowl Add melted butter with eggs onto flour and brown sugar and mix well with a wooden spoon then add grated parmesan cheese( optional ) and mix well.
  3. Pour mix cheese batter and pour onto apple mixture top with additional grated parmesan cheese and bake at 160 C for 45 minutes cover top with foil if top brown too fast.
  4. CRISPY AND PERFECTLY BALANCE OF SWEETNESS AND LIGHTLY SALTY CHEESY PIE CRUST.
