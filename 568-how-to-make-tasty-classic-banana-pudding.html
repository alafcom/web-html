Ingredients of Classic Banana Pudding

  1. It's 2 (3.4 oz) of boxes Vanilla pudding.
  2. Prepare 1 (8 oz) of tub Cool Whip.
  3. Prepare 3 cups of milk.
  4. Prepare 1 (11 oz) of box Nilla Waffers.
  5. It's 3 of large bananas, sliced.

Classic Banana Pudding step by step

  1. Mix puddings and milk, set aside for 5 minutes..
  2. In a large trifle bowl layer the bottom with 1 layer of waffers. Then put a slice of banana on each waffer. *I forgot and my bananas ended up as a single layer on top of the pudding.*.
  3. Mix half the tub of cool whip in to pudding.
  4. Put a quarter of the pudding mixture on top..
  5. Put another layer of waffers along the top and sides. Layer bananas on top of each cookie. *I used a box and a half of 8 oz mini vanilla waffers*.
  6. Add another quarter of pudding mixture. Continue layers til you're done. *I got 2 layers each for my 2 minis.*.
  7. Top the whole thing with cool whip. Chill in the fridge for a few hours..
