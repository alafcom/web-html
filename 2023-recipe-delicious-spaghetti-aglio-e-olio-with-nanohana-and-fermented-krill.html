Spaghetti Aglio e Olio with Nanohana and Fermented Krill step by step

  1. Boil the water for the pasta. Cut the nanohana into 3-4 cm lengths..
  2. Heat olive oil, garlic, and chlli in a frying pan over a low heat until fragrant..
  3. Add the shimeji mushrooms and the thick stalks of the nanohana. Stir well to coat the vegetables with oil (time the pasta so that it's almost done at this step!).
  4. Add the fermented krill, the rest of the nanohana, and a little of the pasta cooking water..
  5. Add the drained pasta and stir well. Sprinkle with black pepper to taste..
