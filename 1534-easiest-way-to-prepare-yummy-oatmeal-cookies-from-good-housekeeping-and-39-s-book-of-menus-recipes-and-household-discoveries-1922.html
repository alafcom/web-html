Oatmeal Cookies from Good Housekeeping's Book of Menus, Recipes and Household Discoveries - 1922 instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Beat eggs.
  3. Sift together flour, cinnamon and salt.
  4. Cream together margarine, shortening and sugar..
  5. Add the beaten eggs , the oats, the sifted flour mixture, the raisins and nuts..
  6. Add the Soda to the vinegar then add to the main mixture..
  7. Mix all together thoroughly, scoop out with a 2 inch scoop then flatten to 1/2 inch thickness, bake on greased and parchment covered cookie sheet for ten minutes in a 400°.F oven..
