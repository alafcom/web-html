Ingredients of Special mango meal

  1. Prepare 2 of chopped mangoes.
  2. It's 1 tbsp of ghee.
  3. You need 1 bowl of rice flour (dardara chawal ka aata).
  4. You need 1/4 of black salt.
  5. It's 1/4 of black pepper.
  6. You need 1/4 tbsp of cumin (jeera).
  7. Prepare 2 pinch of chilli flakes.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of elaichi powder.
  9. You need 5 of Saffron petals (kesar).
  10. It's 1 tbsp of sugar.
  11. It's to taste of salt (according to taste).

Special mango meal instructions

  1. Firstly take chopped mangoes and grinde it and make a puree out of it.
  2. Take a pan and add 2½ bowl water.
  3. Put it on medium flame and add mango puree in it and mix it well.
  4. Add all the spices, saffron, sugar and let it boil.
  5. After a boil add rice flour in it and mix it well.
  6. Then cover it and leaveit on low flame for 1-2 mins and add some ghee to it.
  7. Your dish is ready to serve you can serve it with some mango chutney and ghee on top.
