Ingredients of Dark Chocolate Chocolate Chip Brownie Cookies

  1. You need 1 cup of unsalted cold butter (cut into small cubes).
  2. Prepare 1 cup of dark brown sugar packed.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of sugar.
  4. It's 2 of medium eggs.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of dark baking cocoa.
  6. You need 1 cup of cake flour.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of cornstarch.
  9. You need 1/4 tsp of baking soda.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  11. It's 1 1/3 cup of dark chocolate chips or semi sweet chocolate chips.

Dark Chocolate Chocolate Chip Brownie Cookies step by step

  1. Pre heat oven to 410 degrees..
  2. In a large bowl or stand mixer, cream together cold butter and sugars until light and fluffy, about 4 minutes. Add eggs 1 at time, mixing well after each addition..
  3. Add cocoa, cake flour, all purpose flour, cornstarch, baking soda and salt and stir until combined. Stir in chocolate chips..
  4. Separate dough into large balls and place on a cookie sheet. Lightly press on top of the dough to smooth out..
  5. Bake 9 to 10 minutes until until edges are crisp. Let them rest for 10 minutes..
