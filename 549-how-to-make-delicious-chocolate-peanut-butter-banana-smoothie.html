Ingredients of Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

  1. It's 1 of Banana.
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of Almond Milk, vanilla, unsweetened.
  3. It's 2 tablespoons of Peanut Butter.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of Cacao Powder.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of Ice.
