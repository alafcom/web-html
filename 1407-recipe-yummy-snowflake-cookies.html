Ingredients of Snowflake Cookies

  1. You need 1 c of Butter.
  2. You need 1-8 oz of package of Cream Cheese.
  3. Prepare 1 c of Sugar.
  4. You need 1 of Egg Yolk.
  5. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  6. It's 1 tsp of Orange Rind (grated).
  7. You need 1/4 tsp of Cinnamon.
  8. It's 2 1/2 c of Flour.
  9. It's 1/4 tsp of Salt.

Snowflake Cookies step by step

  1. Cream butter and cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually add sugar, creaming after each addition. Beat in egg yolk, vanilla, orange rind, salt, and cinnamon. Add in flour..
  2. Put dough into cookie press and press the dough onto un-greased cookie sheet. Decorate as you wish..
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-18 minutes..
