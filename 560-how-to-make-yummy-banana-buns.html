Ingredients of Banana buns

  1. Prepare 1 of ripe banana.
  2. You need 3 tbsp of sugar.
  3. You need 1 pinch of salt.
  4. It's 3 tbsp of sour cream.
  5. Prepare 80 ml of vegetable oil.
  6. It's 1 tsp of lemon juice.
  7. Prepare 300 gr of flour.
  8. You need 1 tbsp of soda.
  9. You need 1 tsp of cinnamon (optional).
  10. It's 1/4 cup of grated walnuts (optional).

Banana buns step by step

  1. Mix the ingredients using a hand blender, adding the flour gradually.
  2. Roll the dough and make approx 18-20 buns.
  3. Bake 15-20 mins in 200 degree oven.
