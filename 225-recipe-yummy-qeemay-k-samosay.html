Qeemay k samosay instructions

  1. Sub say phalay qeemay may Pani red chilli crushed aur green chili paste dal kr cook kry.
  2. Jub Pani dry hojaiy to qeemay ko tandha kr lay.
  3. Abb piaz ko cut kr lay anday may Jessi dalty Hy wesay aur Hara dhania podina Hari mirch sub ko barik cut kr lay.
  4. Abb qeemay may sub green maslay aur dry masala dal kr mix kr lay.
  5. Abb samosay patti may yai qeema dal kr samosy bana lay aur oil may deep fry kr lay.
