Ingredients of Ham on a Bun with Heather's Cheese Dip

  1. Prepare of Dip.
  2. Prepare 30 ounces of Ragu's double cheddar cheese.
  3. Prepare 1 pound of ground beef.
  4. Prepare To taste of salt.
  5. It's To taste of ground black pepper.
  6. Prepare To taste of granulated onion powder.
  7. It's To taste of granulated garlic powder.
  8. You need 2 cans of your favorite canned chili.
  9. It's of Ham and bun.
  10. Prepare 1 slice of smoked ham.
  11. You need 1 slice of hotdog bun.

Ham on a Bun with Heather's Cheese Dip step by step

  1. Brown the ground beef add salt, onion powder, pepper, and garlic to taste..
  2. Mix the cheese, chili, and ground beef. Simmer 7 minutes or until warmed through. Add ham to the bun..
  3. Add dip. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
  4. You will have plenty of dip leftover you can make more sandwiches or dip chips in it!!.
