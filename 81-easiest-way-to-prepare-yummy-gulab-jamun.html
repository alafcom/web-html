Ingredients of Gulab jamun

  1. It's 250 gm of Smooth Mawa (khova).
  2. Prepare 50 gm of Maida(all purpose flour).
  3. It's 2 tbsp of rawa.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of paneer(mashed).
  5. It's of Small pinch baking soda.
  6. It's 1 cup of sugar.
  7. It's 2 cups of water.
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of cardamom powder.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of rose water.
  10. You need of Ghee for deep frying.
  11. You need of Few drops lemon juicy lips.

Gulab jamun instructions

  1. In a deep pan,add sugar and water. cook it on medium flame till half-string consistency.sugar syrup should be sticky. Turn off the flame. Add cardamom powder and rose water. Add 4-5 drops of lemon juice to avoid crystallization..
  2. In a big plate take mawa mash it well, add paneer,rawa,baking soda and Maida mix and mash properly. Do not knead it. Make sure dough should be smooth..
  3. Divide dough in equal parts, make small crack free balls.If you feel,Dough is dry add little milk.If dough is over moist then add some Maida..
  4. Heat ghee on medium flame. Place 4-5 gulab jamun balls in ghee. Fry the balls golden brown for 12-15 minutes. Put the hot gulab jamuns into the warm sugar syrup.Gently Stri them..
  5. Let it rest for 30-40 minutes. Serve hot..
