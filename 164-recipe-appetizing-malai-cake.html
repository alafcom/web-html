Ingredients of Malai cake

  1. You need 1 cup of maida.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of malai extracted from milk.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of curd.
  5. You need 1 tsp of baking powder.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  7. It's 1 pinch of salt.
  8. You need 3/4 cup of cream for topping.
  9. You need as needed of Chocolate sauce for topping.
  10. It's As needed of Tuti fruti for topping.

Malai cake instructions

  1. Take sugar, curd and malai in a blending jar and blend smooth to make a batter..
  2. Then sieve maida, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl and mix well. Now, add batter to this mixture and fold to make a smooth batter..
  3. Grease a cake tin with oil pour the mixture and tap well to remove any air bubbles..
  4. Preheat your oven and bake this cake for 30-35 minutes. Do a toothpick taste and when done let it cool. Then Take out in a plate, apply cream covering the cake..pour chocolate sauce and sprinkle tuti fruti..
