Ingredients of Greek Yogurt Blue Cheese Dressing

  1. It's 1-2 cups of crumbled blue cheese.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of buttermilk.
  3. Prepare 1.5 cups of non fat plain greek yogurt.
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons of white vinegar.
  5. You need 2-4 teaspoons of garlic powder.
  6. You need to taste of salt and pepper.
