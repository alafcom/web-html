Banana Bites instructions

  1. Peel your bananas and trim off the ends. Chop the flesh into 6 pieces..
  2. Break the chocolate into a heatproof bowl. Put the bowl over a pan of warm water to melt your chocolate stirring occasionally. Leave to cool slightly..
  3. Push a straw or skewer through the banana pieces, then coat with the chocolate(It should not be perfect).
  4. Roll the banana pieces in coconut or sprinkles. Put then in the frige, wait and serve..
  5. Now have fun eating your banana bites! :).
