Coke cake instructions

  1. Grease and flour a 13×9 cake pan. Preheat oven to 350..
  2. In a saucepan, bring 1/2 cup butter, 1 cup Coke, and oil to a boil. Combine sugar and flour Add to saucepan and stir till well combine..
  3. Remove from heat. Add remaining cake ingredients. Mix well and pour into prepared pan..
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean..
  5. Frosting -Cream butter and 1/2 cup cocoa in a large bowl..
  6. Add in 1/4 cup Coke, chocolate syrup, and 1 tsp vanilla..
  7. Beat in powdered sugar 1/2 cup at a time, scraping down the sides frequently..
  8. If frosting is too dry, add a little Coke to thin. Frost the cake while it is still warm..
  9. Notes- Measure out all ingredients place all ingredients in separate bowls,and set aside.
