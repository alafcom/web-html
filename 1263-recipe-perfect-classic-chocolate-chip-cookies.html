Ingredients of Classic chocolate chip cookies

  1. You need 1 cup of softened butter.
  2. It's 1 cup of white sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of packed brown sugar.
  4. It's 2 of eggs.
  5. You need 1 tsp of baking soda.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of hot water.
  7. You need of ¹/² tsp salt.
  8. Prepare 3 cups of all-purpose flour.
  9. You need 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips.
