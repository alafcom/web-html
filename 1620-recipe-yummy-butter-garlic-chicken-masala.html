Butter Garlic Chicken Masala step by step

  1. In a kadai heat butter and oil togather. Now add a piece of Cinnamon, green cardamom, add 1/2 tsp of cumin seed, 3-4 spoon chopped garlic, 1 tsp of chopped ginger and cook for 10 seconds..
  2. Now add 2 chopped onion,and cook for 1 minute then add 2 chopped tomatoes add salt per taste,1 tsp of coriander powder,1 tsp Garammasala powder,1/2 tsp of Turmeric powder,1 tsp of Kashmiri Red chilli powder, Chopped green chillies add little water. Then add curd and cook for 1 minute..
  3. When the oil gets sepreted add chicken pieces and mix well. Now cover the kadai with a lid and cook for 15 minutes in medium flame..
  4. When chicken is cooked completely.Now add kasuri methi and lots of butter.
  5. Garnish with Chopped coriander..
