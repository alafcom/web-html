Ingredients of Salty yogurt drink - laban ayran

  1. It's 1 kg of yogurt.
  2. You need 3 cups of water.
  3. It's 1 teaspoon of salt.

Known as aryan in Turkey and drunk plain, the cooling, soothing yogurt-based drink is popular all over the Middle East. Syrians and Lebanese call it laban ayran. Great recipe for Salty yogurt drink - laban ayran. Ayran is a healthy and super refreshing yogurt drink.

Salty yogurt drink - laban ayran step by step

  1. Whisk the yogurt well or whip it using an electric mixer then gradually add the water and salt until you get a homogeneous texture..
  2. Serve cold with ice cubes..
