Nenthren pazham sweet instructions

  1. In a pressure pan boil one cup water.
  2. Cut bananas in to cubes add in to the boiled water..
  3. Cook till done.
  4. Add the sugar mix well.
  5. Add coconut milk..
  6. Don't allow to boil.
  7. Remove from heat..
  8. Garnish with chopped roasted cashews and cardamom powder room.
  9. Serve as a dessert..
