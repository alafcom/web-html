Pesto Cheese Toast instructions

  1. Turn oven to broiler..
  2. Place ciabatta on baking sheet cut side down. Broil until bread is toasted, about 3 minutes..
  3. Remove from oven and on the inside of the bread, spoon on 1-2 tbsp pesto on each side. Top with tomato and provolone cheese. Broil until cheese is melted, 4-5 mins..
