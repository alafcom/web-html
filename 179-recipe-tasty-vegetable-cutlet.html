Recipe: Tasty Vegetable cutlet

Vegetable cutlet.

Ingredients of Vegetable cutlet

  1. Prepare 3 of potatoes.
  2. Prepare 1 of carrot.
  3. You need 2-3 of beans.
  4. You need of Small piece of beetroot.
  5. It's of Garam masala.
  6. Prepare of Salt.
  7. Prepare 1 of onion.
  8. Prepare 5-6 of garlic pieces.
  9. Prepare of Chilli powder.
  10. It's of Lemon.
  11. Prepare of Whole wheat Bread.
  12. It's of Suji for coating.
  13. Prepare of Refined oil.

Vegetable cutlet step by step

  1. Boil all the vegetables..
  2. Take a pan heat 1 tablespoon oil, add crushed garlic and saute for 1 minute then add chopped onion till it turns to golden brown then add chilli powder, garam masala and salt..
  3. Take a boil, put all boiled vegetable and smash it well, add the cooked masala, add some more salt, lemon juice and mix it well..
  4. Take 3-4 breads and soak it in water..
  5. Filter the water from breads and add it to the batter we have made earlier and mix it well..
  6. Make the shape that i have shown in my picture..
  7. Then coat it with suji..
  8. Deep fry it until it turns to golden brown..
  9. Serve it will tomato ketchup..
