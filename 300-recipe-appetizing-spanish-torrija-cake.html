Ingredients of Spanish torrija cake

  1. You need 2 of Greek yogurts.
  2. It's 3 of Eggs.
  3. Prepare 80 g of Melted butter.
  4. It's 4 Cups of milk.
  5. You need 2 Cups of Flour (I use Gluten free).
  6. It's 80 g of sugar.
  7. You need 2 Tbsp of instant yeast.
  8. It's 1 Tbsp of Cinnamon powder.
  9. You need 1 Tbsp of Vainilla essence.
