Yummy chocolate filled cookies step by step

  1. Filling: Mix the almond flour, melted chocolate and egg whites (no mixer) until it turns into a grey-brown color mixture. Form small balls and freeze for 2 hours.
  2. Dough: beat butter, add sugars and beat. Mix in flour..
  3. Take out the filling from the freezer. Wrap the dough around the frozen chocolate balls. Make sure they are of a consistent size. Place on cookie sheet..
  4. Bake for 10 minutes on 350Â°. KEEP CHECKING. It bakes easily..
