No fuss Chocolate (sheet) Cake instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients at once as original recipe calls for or follow my steps :.
  2. I sifted the dry ingredients by blending them at a high speed.
  3. And I combined the wet ingredients in the main mixing bowl.
  4. Now add both together and give it a nice stir till no more bubbles of flour appear; tiny clumps at the end are ok.
  5. Pour into lined baking sheet or other cake form and with a spatula make sure to push the liquidy dough into all four corners of your baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 30min in warm oven.
  7. Let cool down.
  8. If you are with me in just loving the whole peanut butter and chocolate thing, then stick with my slight change to the original recipe :.
  9. After having warmed up peanut butter, spread it evenly over top of cake.
  10. Let cool very well.
  11. Now prepare the chocolate glaze :.
  12. Bring sugar, margarine, milk, and cocoa to a boil stirring constantly.
  13. Reduce heat to a simmer for 2 min while stirring.
  14. Remove from heat and stir for another 5 minutes.
  15. Add vanilla, stir, and immediately pour onto cake.
  16. Glaze will dry quickly, so add any sprinkles now.
  17. Let glazed cake cool for an one hour.
