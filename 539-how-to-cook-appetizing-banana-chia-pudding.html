Ingredients of Banana-Chia Pudding

  1. You need 2 of bananas (mashed).
  2. You need 1/3 cup of almond milk.
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp of chia seeds.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of vanilla.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of lemon juice.

Banana-Chia Pudding step by step

  1. Mix the mashed bananas, almond milk, chia seeds, vanilla, and lemon juice together..
  2. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 1.5 hours or overnight. This allows the chia seeds to form a pudding-like texture..
  3. Serve cold with whipped cream and vanilla wafers..
