Sunday's Special Aglio Olio instructions

  1. Slice the garlic, herbs, champignons into small bits..
  2. Boil a pot of water, and add salts. Put the spaghetti. Don't forget to stir once a while..
  3. At the same time, heat (keep it medium) the olive oil in a pan, and put the garlic. Wait (3-4 mins) until it is golden-brown..
  4. Spaghetti is cooked. Dump the water from the spaghetti's pot. Don't forget to take a half cup of the water..
  5. Pour the water into the pan. Put champignons, herbs, and the prawns. Stir a bit..
  6. Mix the sauce into the pot. Add some dried oregano..
  7. Serve the spaghetti..
