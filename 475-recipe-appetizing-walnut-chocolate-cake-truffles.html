Ingredients of Walnut Chocolate Cake Truffles

  1. You need 1 1/2 cup of chocolate cake (crumbs).
  2. Prepare 1 cup of chocolate to melt in bar (chopped) or pearls.
  3. Prepare 6 tablespoons of heavy whipping cream.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of butter.
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon of dulce de leche (not pastry).
  6. It's of Chocolate liqueur or any liqueur (to taste) (optional).
  7. You need of Chopped nuts (necessary amount).
  8. Prepare of Chocolate chips (necessary amount).
  9. Prepare of Coconut flakes (necessary amount).
  10. Prepare Tablespoon of with rounded shape (measure).
  11. You need cups of Paper candy.

To melt the chocolate: To bain marie: Place boiling water in a pot At low temperature maintain the water temperature Place inside the pot a container with the chocolate inside it, the container should not touch the bottom and that is not be submerged in the water When it starts to melt stir until the chocolate is completely melted.
  4. In the microwave: Place the chocolate in a container in the microwave, melt it at maximum temperature for 30 seconds Remove, stir, put back 15 seconds Repeat periods of 15 minutes, removing from microwave and stirring until the chocolate is completely melted Tip. If the optimum point of melting is accidentally exceeded, you can recover the chocolate by adding pearls or chocolate fragments by mixing them until they are melted in the chocolate.
  5. Add and mix the cake in small portions with the melted chocolate.
  6. Add the dulce de leche, mix Add the liquor (optional) Refrigerate for one hour.
