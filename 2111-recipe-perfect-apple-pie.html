Apple Pie.

You can have Apple Pie using 11 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Apple Pie

  1. It's 4 each of Granny Smith apples (tart).
  2. You need 4 each of Fuji Apples (sweet).
  3. Prepare 2 each of Lemon's Juice (1/2 cup).
  4. It's 3/4 cup of Sugar (white).
  5. It's 1/2 cup of Brown sugar.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of All purpose Flour.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Nutmeg.
  9. It's 1 pinch of Ground cloves.
  10. It's 2 tbsp of Cubed butter.
  11. Prepare 2 of Pie crust- ready or homemade.

Apple Pie step by step

  1. Slice apples. After the first few slices, pour the lemon juice in the bowl with Apples. This keeps apples from browning while you are slicing..
  2. Add the remaining 6 ingredients. The mixtures will begin to moisten as you fold ingredients. Press crust in pie pan. Pour mixture in. Place pats of butter around mixture. Top with the second crust. Seal. Slice 3 small slits on the top crust..
  3. Bake at 375°F until crust is golden brown and mixture is bubbling, one hour. Place a long pan underneath pie plate to catch overflow..
