Hyderabadi Bagara Baingan step by step

  1. Half cut Eggplant from both ends. Take oil in a pan. Flame should be medium..
  2. Shallow Fry eggplants till golden brown colour..
  3. .
  4. Step-1. Dry roast all ingredients now. Ginger, Onion, Garlic Cloves.
  5. Completed..
  6. Step-2. Dry roast Coconut Powder till it become golden brown.
  7. Coconut Powder, Peanuts, Coriander Seeds.
  8. Add to dry roast - Poppy Seeds, White Sesame Seeds, Whole Red Chilli.
  9. .
  10. Step-3. Grind the mixture.
  11. Take Peanut oil in a pan. Shallow Fry Mustard Seeds, Fenugreek Seeds, Curry Leaves until it Crackles..
  12. Add the mixture prepared in Step-3. Add little water to avoid burning of mixture..
  13. Roast this mixture till oil gets separate..
  14. Add Tamarind Pulp and Jaggery at this stage. Start adding spices - Kashmiri Lal Mirch, Salt..
  15. Add water to the mixture so that consistency become thin..
  16. Now add fried eggplant to this gravy. Cook until gravy become thick. You can also eggplant later before serving. It's your choice..

  18. Serve HOT.
