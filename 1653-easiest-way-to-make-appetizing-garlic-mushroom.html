Garlic mushroom instructions

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. add 2 tsp finely chopped garlic... ginger. saute on a medium flame for half a minute..
  2. Then add 3 tbsp finely chopped onion. saute for a minute. now add 1/2 tsp pepper powder.stir the mixture..
  3. Now add sliced or chopped white button mushrooms. first the mushrooms would release a lot of water. then add 1 tbs soy sauce. & 1/2 tsp vinegar..
  4. Next add 1tsp red chilli powder.u can add little water.then add salt & mix well..nw ready 2 serve.
