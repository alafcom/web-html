Ingredients of Brioche Bread (bread maker)

  1. It's 2 cups of Bread Flour.
  2. You need 180 ml of Milk.
  3. It's 20 g of Butter (melted).
  4. You need 1 of large Egg (60g).
  5. You need 30 g of Sugar.
  6. Prepare 2 g of Salt.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Yeast.

Brioche Bread (bread maker) step by step

  1. Add milk, egg and melted butter into the bread pan..
  2. Add salt and sugar..
  3. Add flour and make a small hole to pour the yeast (keep the yeast away from the salt)..
  4. Insert the bread pan to the bread machine..
  5. Use Soft bread menu, 500 g and dark crust (2 hours and 25 minutes)..
  6. When bread is done let the loaf cool on a wire rack for about 30 minutes..
