Ingredients of Gamberi aglio, olio e peperoncino

  1. You need of Enough king prawns per person.
  2. You need 2-3 cloves of garlic.
  3. It's of Good amount of olive oil.
  4. You need 1-2 of fresh chillies.
  5. You need of Fresh parsley and crusty bread to serve.

Gamberi aglio, olio e peperoncino step by step

  1. Prep the prawns buy removing the head, cutting down the spine and taking out the vein. Wash and dry well.
  2. Fry the garlic in olive oil for 2-3 mins.
  3. Add the chopped chillies and cook for another min.
  4. Add the prawns. Cook on medium.
  5. When the prawns are pink, add some parsley, remove garlic and serve :).
