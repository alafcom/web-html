Ingredients of Bucatini aglio e olio

  1. You need 1 box of bucatini noodles (use any pasta you prefer).
  2. Prepare 8 cloves of garlic chopped.
  3. It's 1 tbsp of crushed red pepper.
  4. You need 1 bunch of italian parsley chopped.
  5. Prepare 1/2 pound of butter.
  6. You need 1 of lemon.
  7. Prepare 4 oz of fresh parmesan grated (or you can buy grated or shaved).

Bucatini aglio e olio instructions

  1. Bring salted water in a pot to a boil and cook your bucatini noodles for about 8 minutes..
  2. In a pan, melt your butter.
  3. Add your chopped garlic and lightly toast it (will look lightly browned).
  4. Turn the heat off your butter.
  5. Add your parsley and crushed red pepper.
  6. Squeeze the juice from the lemon.
  7. Add your pasta to the butter and mix.
  8. Grate your parmesan on top. (Best to do after each serving).
