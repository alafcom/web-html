Ingredients of Keto Macadamia Butter Cookies

  1. You need 1.5 C of homemade or purchased Macadamia Butter.
  2. Prepare 1/3 C of Ground Almond Flour.
  3. Prepare to taste of Substitute sugar (Stevia liquid, xylitol, erythritol).
  4. It's 2 L of Eggs.
  5. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of cinnamon.
