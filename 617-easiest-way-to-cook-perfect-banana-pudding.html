Banana Pudding ðŸ˜‹ðŸ˜‹ ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽ‰ instructions

  1. Cut up bananas and squeeze lemon juice on it, give them a toss and cover. Lemon juice keeps bananas from turning black ðŸ˜„.
  2. Separate egg yolks and place in a bowl also soften the cream cheese in the microwave.
  3. Pour the milk into a pan on the stove at medium heat.
  4. Add the yolks and WHISK into the milk.
  5. Add the flour, sugar and salt, put through a sifter then add to milk mixture and WHISK constantly.
  6. Keep whisking until starting to get thick and add 2 tsp instant french vanilla pudding this is better than adding more flour.
  7. Thickening might take 10-15 minutes but go ahead and add the block of cream cheese while the custard is hot and keep whisking until it is all dissolved and your sauce is thick.
  8. Take off the heat and add the vanilla extract whisking it in then let cool for about 10 minutes.
  9. Start your layers !! Add a little custard to the bottom of the pan then add a layer of Nilla wafers.
  10. Then add your sliced bananas on top of the wafers then do another layer the same way.
