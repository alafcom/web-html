stuffed red bell peppers~adapted from big bob Gibson BBQ book instructions

  1. preheat cooker to 400.
  2. in a small frying pan sauteed onion and bacon together for 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. add garlic sauteed for 30 seconds.
  4. drain bacon grease, keep.
  5. add beans, brown sugar, maple syrup,BBQ sauce, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce and yellow mustard in bacon onion mixture.
  6. with a pairing knife, slice off each top of peppers, core out seeds, veins and core.
  7. fill each pepper with 3/4 cup of bean mixture.
  8. place peppers on void side of grill, close like cook over indirect heat for 50 minutes.
