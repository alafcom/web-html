Ingredients of Persimmon Cookies

  1. Prepare 2 Cups of flour.
  2. It's 1 Cup of butter or margarine (room temperature).
  3. You need 1 of egg.
  4. Prepare 1 Cup of sugar.
  5. You need 1 Tbsp of cinnamon.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of nutmeg.
  7. You need 1/2 of all spice.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  9. You need 1 tsp of salt.
  10. Prepare 1 Tbsp of vanilla.
  11. You need 1 of glob of honey.
  12. You need 1 Cup of diced persimmon.
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup of dried cranberries.
  14. Prepare 1/2 Cup of pecan pieces.

Persimmon Cookies step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350. Line sheet with parchment paper..
  2. Sift flour, salt, spices, and baking soda.
  3. In separate bowl cream sugar and butter together. Add egg and vanilla. Slowly mix into flour mixture..
  4. Mix persimmon, cranberries, honey, pecans together then gently fold into batter..
  5. Scoop batter into balls and place them evenly spaced on baking sheet..
  6. Baking at 350 for 14 minutes or until the edges are golden brown..
  7. Let cool on baking sheet.
