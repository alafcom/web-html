Easy Pudding cake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 450F.
  2. Make the custard liquid. Mix the eggs and sugar well. And add the milk in a bowl..
  3. Cut the bread into pieces(one slice into 9 pieces).
  4. Coat the container with butter, pour half of the liquid..
  5. Place the bread pieces and let them soak the custard..
  6. Pour the rest of the liquid before sending it into the oven. Bake about 5 mins..
  7. Pull out and add some raisins and bake for another 10 mins..
