Badam pista matka kulfi step by step

  1. Pour milk into a pan and keep it for boil on high flame.
  2. In a bowl take 3 spoon water n add corn starch n mix it well.
  3. Wen milk comes to boil keep d flame on medium n add starch mixture n stir d milk continously.
  4. Wen d 60% quantity left in pan add sugar, mix well n stir it till it melts, now add badam n pista into it n mix well.
  5. Now switch off d flame n let d mixture cool down.
  6. Wen it cools completely keep it in refrigerator for 1 hour den whisk it for 5 minutes, u may also grind it into mixer.
  7. Take small mitti ka matka n fill it with kulfi mixture n sprinkle badam n pista over it.. freeze it till it sets properly coverings with sling.
  8. Enjoy ur summer with this cool cool matka kulfi.
