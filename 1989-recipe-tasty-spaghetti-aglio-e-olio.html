Spaghetti aglio e olio instructions

  1. Finely slice 6-7 cloves of garlic. Chop parsley and cut the bell peppers in dice shape.
  2. Boil the spaghetti.
  3. In a pan add olive oil and garlic together when the pan in cold. Turn on the flame and Keep it on low. • After 2-3 minutes add the chilli flakes. • When you see garlic changing it’s colour add the vegetables and stir..
  4. Then quickly add the boiled spaghetti. • Add salt, white pepper (or black pepper) and chopped parsley and serve..
  5. Voila! Enjoy your day cooking this easy peasy recipe for your family..
