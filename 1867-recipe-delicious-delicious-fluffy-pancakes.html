Ingredients of Delicious Fluffy Pancakes

  1. You need 140 grams of all-purpose flour (1 cup).
  2. It's 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder.
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda.
  4. You need 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  5. You need 1 tablespoon of sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 of large egg.
  7. Prepare 250 grams of milk.
  8. You need 1 tablespoon of vinegar (you can substitute butter milk for the milk and vinegar if you have it).
  9. Prepare 30 grams of butter, melted.

Delicious Fluffy Pancakes step by step

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar in a large bowl..
  2. Separate the eggs, and beat the egg whites with a hand mixer until it gets a bit stiff (you'll get peaks when you pull the beaters out of the bowl).
  3. In another bowl, mix the egg yolks with the milk and vinegar (or use buttermilk if you have it). Then drizzle the melted butter on top of the yolk mixture, and stir in..
  4. Add the egg whites to the egg yolk mixture and fold it in carefully, until it's combined. Then pour on top of the dry mixture and whisk to combine..
  5. Depending on if you want it thicker, or wetter you can add either more flour or more milk.
  6. Heat a frying pan on the stove at medium heat, and grease with some additional butter (or use your favourite oil as an alternative). Then pour some of the mix into the pan. You can make them any size your want..
  7. Once they start to bubble slightly, you can flip them over. Once they go a bit brown on both sides, transfer to a plate. Now add some more butter to the pan, and repeat until they're all finished. You might have to turn the heat down as you cook more to avoid cooking them too fast..
  8. If you make all of the pancakes slightly different sizes, then you can stack them like a pyramid on your plate, so the maple syrup will fall off the edges like it's going down a staircase. Enjoy! ☺️.
