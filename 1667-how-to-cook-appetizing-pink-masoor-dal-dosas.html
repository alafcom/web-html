Pink Masoor Dal Dosas instructions

  1. Wash both the Dals and the rice well..
  2. Soak the dals & rice together in water in a deep vessel. Cover & set aside for 7-8 hours..
  3. Drain the water. Add the Poha. Grind in batches in a mixer jar, adding water little by little, as required to make a smooth batter..
  4. Cover & set aside the ground batter in a warm place to ferment for 6-7 hours..
  5. If you want, you can add rice flour, add the eno, salt & adjust the consistency of the batter by adding water and make Dosas instantly. I prefer the natural fermentation process.
  6. After fermentation, add rice flour, salt and water to get the desired, runny consistency..
  7. Heat a Dosa tava or a nonstick tava, spread a 1 TSP of oil, sprinkle some drops of water..
  8. Pour a ladleful of the ready batter. Spread into a circle. Cook till golden brown from one side. Drizzle some more oil or Ghee on the sides..
  9. Flip and cook from the other side until golden brown and crisp..
  10. Serve hot with Sambar and Chutney of your choice..
