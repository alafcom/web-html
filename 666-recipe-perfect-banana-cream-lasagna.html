Banana Cream Lasagna step by step

  1. Crush oreos and mix with 8 tbls melted butter.
  2. Spread to cover bottom of 9x13 baking dish.
  3. In bowl mix cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, 8oz cool whip. Mix well and cover crust mixture..
  4. In another bowl mix pudding mix and milk. Cover cream cheese layer and refrigerate at least 5 minutes..
  5. Top with rest of cool whip and add some more crushed vanilla wafers..
  6. Refrigerate at least 1 hour..
