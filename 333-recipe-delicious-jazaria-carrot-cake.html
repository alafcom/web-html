Ingredients of “Jazaria” Carrot Cake

  1. Prepare of Cake.
  2. It's 3/4 cup of vegetable oil.
  3. You need 1 cup of light brown sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of regular white sugar.
  5. You need 3 of Large eggs (4 Medium).
  6. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla extract.
  7. You need 2 Cups of all-purpose flour sifted.
  8. Prepare 2 tsp of baking powder.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  10. It's 1 tsp of cinnamon powder.
  11. You need 1/2 tsp of ground allspice (optional).
  12. It's 2 cups of loosely packed, finely grated carrots.
  13. It's 3/4 cup of lightly toasted walnut pieces (you can replace with raisins).
  14. It's of Frosting Ingredients.
  15. Prepare 225 g of cream cheese at room temperature.
  16. Prepare 1/4 cup of unsalted butter at room temperature.
  17. Prepare 3 cup of icing sugar “sifted”.
  18. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  19. It's 1 tsp of lemon juice.
  20. Prepare of Lightly roasted walnuts pieces for garnish.

“Jazaria” Carrot Cake instructions

  1. Whisk your wet ingredients with the sugars..
  2. Sift all your dry ingredients and add the carrots with the dry mix..
  3. Mix your dry to the wet mix. Then add roasted walnuts and mix again..
  4. Divide the mix in two equal size pans and bake for 28-30 mins at 175c (350f). Make sure when you insert a skewer it comes out clean..
  5. Add three frost mix between your cake layers and in top and on sides. Garnish with roasted walnuts and refrigerate for 90 mins. Enjoy after.
