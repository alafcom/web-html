Chicken Sorrel Curry step by step

  1. Clean chicken, marinate and keep it aside for 1 hour..
  2. Take a pan pour oil add seasoning ingredients saute till light brown..
  3. Add Onions saute till translucent..
  4. Add turmeric powder, salt, chilli powder and saute till all are mixed..
  5. Add marinated chicken saute till chicken slightly changes the colour..
  6. Add puree and mix well..
  7. Add Coriander powder, chicken masala, Coriander leaves, close the lid and pressure cook for 2 whistles..
  8. Now Chicken Sorrel leaves curry is ready to be served..
