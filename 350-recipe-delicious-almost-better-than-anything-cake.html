Ingredients of Almost better than anything cake

  1. Prepare 1 box of devils food cake (plus ingredients called for on package).
  2. It's 1 can of sweetened condensed milk.
  3. You need 8 ounces of caramel sundae topping.
  4. You need 1 tub of cool whip.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of heath bits.

Almost better than anything cake step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350, prepare cake mix and bake as directed. Let it cool for 10 minutes..
  2. While cake is cooling mix together caramel topping and sweetened condensed milk in a sauce pan on low heat..
  3. Using the handle of a wooden spoon poke holes every 1/2 inch of the cake and pour the caramel milk mixture over the top. Sprinkle 1 cup of the heath bits over top and place in fridge for 2 hours..
  4. Top with cool whip and drizzle with some of the caramel topping and sprinkle heath bits on top..
