Turmeric Choco Cake instructions

  1. Using a blender or nutribullet mix all ingredients aside from almond flour..
  2. Add almond flour then mix it again using blender or nutribullet.
  3. Place aluminum foil or baking sheet inside the rice cooker and melt 2tbsp of butter if using aluminum foil..
  4. Pour in the mixture. Press cook every 15minutes for 4 times..
  5. Remove the cake from the cooker and remove the foil..
  6. Put the vanilla yogurt on top of the cake for added moisture..
