Cocoa-banana oatmeal step by step

  1. Put 2 cups of water in a saucepan and bring it to a boil..
  2. After the water starts boiling add the rolled oats and the cocoa and stir the mixture. Cover the saucepan and reduce heat to low..
  3. After the oats are tender and the water is absorbed add the cooked oatmeal in a bowl..
  4. Add chopped banana and one tablespoon of honey on top. Voila.
