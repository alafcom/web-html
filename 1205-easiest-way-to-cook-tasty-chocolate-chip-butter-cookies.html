Ingredients of Chocolate chip butter cookies

  1. You need 500 g of salted butter.
  2. You need 1 teaspoon of vanilla essence.
  3. You need 175 g of caster sugar.
  4. It's 1 of egg.
  5. Prepare 450 g of Plain flour/ Top flour.
  6. It's 450 g of choc chip.

Chocolate chip butter cookies instructions

  1. Beat soften butter then add in egg and vanilla essence.
  2. Add sugar, beat until mixture become pale.
  3. Add flour and mix well. Then add in choc chip. Done!.
  4. Transfer your dough into piping bag if you like it neat. You may want to chill your dough for 30 minutes in the fridge. Dough can also last up to 6 months in a freezer..
  5. Pipe out your cookie mixture onto tray. While doing that make sure you have preheated your oven at 170°C for 20 minutes.
  6. Bake cookies for 15-20 minutes. Depending on your oven.
