Ingredients of Mango chocolate smoothie

  1. It's 1 cup of mango pulp.
  2. You need 2 tbs of sugar.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of fresh cream.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of Chopped Mango slices for garnishing.
  5. You need of Chocolate syrup.
  6. It's 1 tbs of butter.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of dark compound chocolate.
  8. It's 1 tbs of coco powder.
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup of fresh cream.

Mango chocolate smoothie step by step

  1. Take a grinder bowl add mango pulp sugar and cream. Grind only for 30 sec and refrigerate for cooling..
  2. For chocolate syrup: keep a pan on medium flame add butter fresh cream and switch off the flame add chocolate and let it melt add coco powder and mix well. Will turn to a smooth chocolate syrup..
  3. Now take a serving glass pour mango smoothie 3/4th glass and add chocolate syrup and pour some more smoothie on top and garnish with chopped mango pieces. Serve chilled..
