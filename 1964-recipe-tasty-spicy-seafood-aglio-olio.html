Ingredients of Spicy seafood aglio olio

  1. Prepare 250 gram of spaghetti.
  2. Prepare 2 tbs of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 8 clove of garlic (chopped).
  4. Prepare of Seafood mix on choice (squid, prawns or mussels).
  5. Prepare of Chicken sausage (chopped into small bites).
  6. You need 1/3 cup of water.
  7. You need 2 tsp of chilli flakes.
  8. Prepare to taste of Salt and blackpepper.
  9. Prepare of Spices mix on choice (oregano, rosemary, parsley and basil).
  10. You need 1/2 cube of anchovies stock (*optional).
  11. You need 1/2 cup of grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese (*optional).
