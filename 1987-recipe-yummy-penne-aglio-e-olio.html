Ingredients of Penne Aglio E Olio

  1. Prepare 100 g of wheat penne pasta.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of garlic.
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of chilli flakes & oregano or Italian seasoning.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of parmesan cheese.
  6. It's to taste of Salt.

Penne Aglio E Olio instructions

  1. Boil wheat penne pasta with salt till it's cooked 95%. Run it thru water and drain. Add 1 tsp olive oil and keep aside..
  2. In a pan take 1 tbsp o fb Olive oil. Add finely chopped garlic and bell peppers(optional) on a low flame heat..
  3. Add penne pasta and toss lightly. Add salt and finish with Italian dressing or chilli flakes and oregano. Optional - Add 2 slices of cheese slices.
  4. Finish with grated Parmesan and serve hot..
