Ingredients of Spaghetti Aglio E Olio

  1. You need 250 g of Spaghetti.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of good extra virgin olive oil.
  3. It's 1 cup of grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of chilli flakes.
  5. Prepare Head of garlic thinly sliced.
  6. You need 2 of lemons.
  7. You need 3/4 cup of (loosely packed) Fresh Continental Parsley diced.
  8. It's of Sea salt or cooking salt.
  9. Prepare of Black pepper.

Spaghetti Aglio E Olio step by step

  1. Cook Spaghetti in salted water until al dente. (Save 1 1/2 cups of the pasta water for later)..
  2. On medium low heat, heat up the olive oil in a skillet, when heated add the chilli flakes and garlic until it begins to brown..
  3. Add pasta water and turn up heat till 1/3 of it boils away..
  4. Add the juice of one lemon and then take the skillet off heat..
  5. Place pasta in skillet and mix in 3/4 of the cheese, all of the parsley and the juice of half a lemon. Add salt and pepper to taste (add more lemon or chilli to taste if you’d like).
  6. Serve topped with the rest of the cheese..
