Ingredients of Apple Pie Baked Beans

  1. Prepare 48 oz of jar great northern beans (or your choice) (drained).
  2. It's 4 tbs of butter.
  3. You need 2 of large baking apples - peeled and diced.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  5. You need 1/4 cup of white sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of apple pie spice (or nutmeg).
  8. It's 1/8 tsp of red pepper flakes (optional).
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of salt.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp of ground black pepper.
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of ketchup.
  12. Prepare 1-2 tbs of molasses.
  13. It's 1 tsp of apple cider vinegar.
  14. It's 4-5 slices of bacon.

Apple Pie Baked Beans step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Spray a 2-quart casserole dish with cooking spray..
  3. In large skillet, melt butter, add diced apples and diced onion then cook until tender (about 5 minutes)..
  4. Add brown sugar, white sugar, cinnamon, red pepper flakes, salt & pepper, stir until well combined..
  5. Cook until sugar is melted..
  6. Stir in ketchup, molasses, vinegar. Stir well..
  7. Add beans and mix well..
  8. Place bacon slices over the top..
  9. Pour mixture into the prepared 2 quart casserole..
  10. Bake in a 400F oven for about 45 mins-1 hour..
  11. Crock Pot Instructions: Spray the crock pot with cooking spray. Sautee apples and onions in a large skillet then place all ingredients in a crock pot or slow-cooker. Cook on high 2-3 hours or low 4 hours..
