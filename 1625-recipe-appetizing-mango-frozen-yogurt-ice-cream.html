Ingredients of Mango frozen yogurt ice cream

  1. It's 1 of big mango.

  4. Prepare of Few dry fruits and tutti fruity (optional).

Mango frozen yogurt ice cream step by step

  1. Wash peel and cut mango in cubes, then take a grinder and add mango, curd and honey..
  2. Grind all them together. Transfer the mixture in bowl and add dry fruits and tutti fruity and mix well..
  3. Now take a any container where we want to set frozen yogurt ice cream and pour this mixture in container. Sprinkle some more dry fruits, tutti fruity and set in refrigerator for 5-6 hour..
  4. Our mango frozen yogurt ice is ready for the serve..
