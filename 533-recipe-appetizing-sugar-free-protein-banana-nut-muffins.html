Ingredients of Sugar free protein banana nut muffins

  1. You need 1/2 cup (1 stick) of soft or melted butter.
  2. You need 3 of large ripe bananas.
  3. You need 1 of egg.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of salt.
  5. It's 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  6. You need 1 tsp of baking powder.
  7. You need 1 tsp of baking soda.
  8. You need 3/4 cup of Splenda (or 1/2 cup sugar if you don’t want sugar free).
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 scoops of unflavored whey protein powder.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of AP flour.
  11. You need 3/4 cup of chopped walnuts and/or chocolate chips.
  12. It's of Equal parts cinnamon and sugar sprinkled on top before baking.
