Ingredients of Banana Oatmeal Smoothie

  1. It's 1/4 cup of old fashioned oats.
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup of milk (any kind, I use an almond-coconut blend).
  3. You need 2 of spotty bananas.
  4. It's 2 tbsp. of peanut butter or almond butter.
  5. You need 1/2 tsp. of ground cinnamon.
  6. You need 1 tsp. of ground flaxseed (optional).
  7. You need 1 tsp. of hemp seeds (optional).
  8. It's 2-3 of ice cubes.

Banana Oatmeal Smoothie step by step

  1. Start the night before, by soaking the oats. Place the oats with the milk in a mason jar or Tupperware with a lid and refrigerate overnight..
  2. In the morning when you're ready to make the smoothie, place the soaked oats and milk into the blender, then add the rest of the ingredients..
  3. Blend until smooth and enjoy immediately..
