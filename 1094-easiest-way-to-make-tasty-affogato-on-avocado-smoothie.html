Ingredients of Affogato on Avocado Smoothie

  1. You need 1 of small avocado, pit removed.
  2. Prepare 20 g of condensed milk, adjust to taste.
  3. Prepare 150 ml of water.
  4. It's 45 g of ice cubes.
  5. You need of chocolate syrup/chocolate condensed milk for drizzle.
  6. Prepare 1 of large scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  7. Prepare 1 shot of espresso / strong brewed coffee.
