Ingredients of Gios cookies

  1. Prepare 1/2 cup of Olivio light brand butter.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of fine blended Florida crystals Cane sugar.
  3. It's 1 1/2 cup of pf All purpose flour.
  4. It's 1 of large egg.
  5. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract.
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp of Baking Powder.
  7. It's 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of fine blended Old fashioned Quaker Oats.

Gios cookies instructions

  1. I use a food processor to make everything. First mix butter and sugar..
  2. Add the egg, vanilla, cinnamon and mix..
  3. Then add the blended oats along with the baking powder..
  4. Then add the flour and mix. Note: If too sticky add just a tad bit of flour and finish mixing..
  5. Refrigerate for 1 hour then with a spoon at a time take the dough and make small balls with your hand. If dough starts sticking to hands wash your hands and dry and continue.. placing them on wax paper..
  6. At 350F cook for 15-17mins.
