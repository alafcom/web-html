Ingredients of cozy chicken and rice soup

  1. It's 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. Prepare 12 oz of boneless chicken thighs.
  3. It's 1 of leek.
  4. You need 2 of stock celery diced.
  5. It's 2 of carrots diced.
  6. You need 1 of potato, peeled & diced.
  7. Prepare 3 clove of thyme & parsley.
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp of salt and pepper.
  9. Prepare 4 cup of chicken broth.
  10. You need 1/2 cup of basmati rice.
  11. It's 1 cup of frozen peas.

cozy chicken and rice soup step by step

  1. add peek and celery to pan. cook over med heat until soft. aprox 2 mins. add carrots, potato, thyme, parsley,s&p. cook, stirring often for 3 mins.
  2. heat oil over med-high heat. brown chicken. remove and cut into chunks. set aside.
  3. return chicken to pot. stir in broth and 4 cups of water. bring to boil. reduce heat and simmer 5 mins..
  4. stir in rice, cook until veggies and rice are tender. about 13 mins. add peas. discard thyme and parsley.
