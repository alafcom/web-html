Mac & Cheese: The Simple Edition step by step

  1. Prepare in the table: Pasta, Milk, salt, pepper, and cheese.
  2. Shred Cheddar Cheese(240g).
  3. Prepare pot and warm it up; High Fire.
  4. Pour Milk(1L).
  5. Boil.
  6. Put the Pasta And Reduce heat!.
  7. Put 1/4 Teaspoon of Pepper and Saltq.
  8. (LOW HEAT!!) Stir and stir for 10-12 minutes.
  9. After 10-12 minutes, Remove from heat.
  10. Put Cheese and mix and mix.
