Ingredients of Chocolate Coconut No-Bake Cookies

  1. It's 2 1/2 cups of quick oats.
  2. You need 2 cups of sugar.
  3. You need 1 stick of butter.
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup of baking Cocoa.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of sweetened Coconut flakes.
  6. It's 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.

Chocolate Coconut No-Bake Cookies instructions

  1. In a largish saucepan, bring sugar, butter, cocoa, and milk to a boil. Let boil 5 min stirring to combine..
  2. After boiling for 5 min, remove from heat, add oats, Coconut, and vanilla extract, and stir to combine..
  3. Put spoonfuls onto wax paper or foil and allow to set for 45 min to an hour..
