Slime Drink instructions

  1. Combine jelly powder with 1 cup of hot water. Mix well until the jelly powder and sugar dissolve..
  2. Add 7-up/Sprite and mix well. Stir until well combined..
  3. Store in the fridge for at least 8 hours before serving. The drink will be gooey and smooth..
