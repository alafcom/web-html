Cheese bread step by step

  1. Pour milin a pan. Put butter in it. Heat the bowl at the microwave for 30 seconds..
  2. Add active yeast into the bowl and mix it and melt butter..
  3. Put 1/3 bread flour in the bowl and beat until smooth..
  4. Put 2/3 bread flour and pizza cheese in the bowl. Mix it to form a soft dough..
  5. Heat the bowl at 200C microwave for 30 seconds..
  6. Cover the bowl with the wet cooking sheet on and let rise in a warm place until doubled for 30 minutes..
  7. Punch dough down. And divide the dough into 6 pieces and make them round..
  8. Put the each dough on a plate and heat it on 200C microwave..
  9. Cover the plate with a wet cooking sheet and let rise in a warm place until doubled for 30minutes..
  10. Bake the dough on 180C oven for 15 minutes..
