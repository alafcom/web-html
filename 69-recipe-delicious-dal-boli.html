Ingredients of Dal boli

  1. Prepare 250 gm of maida.
  2. Prepare 150 g of Bengal gram Dhall.
  3. You need 150 g of jaggery.
  4. You need 2 tablespoon of cardamom powder.
  5. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  6. Prepare 150 g of ghee.

Dal boli instructions

  1. Take a bowl put maida and salt ghee and water to it and make a dough,wrap it with a cloth and keep it for 2 hours.
  2. Soak Bengal gram Dhall for 1/2 an hour and cook it in a pressure cooker till becomes soft.
  3. Grind the Dhall once cooled.
  4. Mix jaggery with water and filter the dust particles.
  5. Take a pan add jaggery syrup and make a string consistency then add grind Bengal gram Dhall cardamom powder sauté well till get a thick stuffing.
  6. Take a maida dough like a ball and spread it on a plantain leaf with ghee and keep a Bengal gram Dhall dough in centre of it and close it well and so that stuffing fully covered and gently pressing it over a leaf.
  7. Take a dosa pan add ghee once get heat place the boli on tawa,cook in a medium flame so that both the sides cook well.
  8. Once done serve it with little ghee spread over on it,it’s a traditional delicious dessert and everyone will like it.
