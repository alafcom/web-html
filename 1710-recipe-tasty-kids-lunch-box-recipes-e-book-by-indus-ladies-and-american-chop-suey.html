Kids Lunch box recipes e-book by Indus ladies and American Chop Suey step by step

  1. Take a pan. Mix soya sauce,vinegar,Chill sauce,tomato sauce and corn flour. Keep it on slow flame boil until the sauce thickens an add 1 tsp pepper powder..
  2. Boil noodles and drain water. Spread them on a wide plate and let it cool down for 5 minutes. In a big tawa add oil and saute noodles until crispy..
  3. Veggies.
  4. Take which ever veggies you prefer. I took Cauliflower,broccoli,beans,carrot and capsicum..
  5. Heat 3 tbsp of oil and add chopped onions. Saute for a while. Add all the veggies and saute on a high heat. Sprinkle ajinamoto a pinch. Saute for a while and sprinkle salt..
  6. Add these veggies in the sauce..
  7. When serving. Pour the above sauce on the fried noodles and serve it individually..
